Gold Trading Analysis: Technical Outlook and Short-Term Forecast

Gold has been a popular commodity for traders due to its historical value and its use as a hedge against inflation and economic uncertainty. In recent weeks, gold has seen a surge in demand as investors seek safe-haven assets amid global economic uncertainties.
