AMZN (AMZN) Trading Analysis

Amazon (AMZN) is a well-known and highly traded stock in the technology sector. As of the time of writing, the stock is trading at $236.59, with a 24-hour change of -3.20%. Market Overview

Amazon's stock has been on a downward trend in the past week, with a 5-day high of $251.75 and a 5-day low of $232.89. Technical Analysis

Looking at the technical indicators for AMZN, the stock is currently trading below its 50-day and 200-day moving averages, indicating a bearish sentiment. Trading Outlook

Based on the current market conditions and technical analysis, the short-term trading forecast for AMZN is neutral to bearish. The stock has found support at the $232.89 level, but it is currently trading below its 5-day high and both its moving averages. On the other hand, a break below the $232.89 support level could lead to further downside movement.
