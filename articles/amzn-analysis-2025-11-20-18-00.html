Amazon (AMZN) Trading Analysis

Amazon (AMZN) is a stock that has been experiencing a bearish trend in the market recently. As of the time of writing, the current price of AMZN is $219.09, with a 24-hour change of -7.78%. AMZN has been on a downward trend for the past 5 days, with the 5-day high at $238.73 and the 5-day low at $218.22.
