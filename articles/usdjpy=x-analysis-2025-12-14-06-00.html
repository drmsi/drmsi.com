USD/JPY Trading Analysis

Introduction

The USD/JPY is a popular currency pair in the Forex market, representing the exchange rate between the US dollar and the Japanese yen. In recent years, this pair has seen significant volatility due to global economic events and political tensions. In this analysis, we will take a closer look at the current state of the USD/JPY and provide a short-term trading outlook.

The USD/JPY is currently trading at $155.75, with a 24-hour change of 0.26%. The trend for this pair is currently bullish, with a 5-day high of $156.92 and a 5-day low of $154.90. The support level for the USD/JPY is at $154.90, while the resistance level is at $156.92.
