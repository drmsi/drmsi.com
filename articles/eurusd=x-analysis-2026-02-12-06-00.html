EUR/USD Trading Analysis: Bullish Trend Continues with Resistance at $1.19

The EUR/USD (EURUSD=X) pair has been on a bullish trend in recent days, with a current price of $1.19 and a 24-hour change of 0.76%. This upward movement has been driven by a combination of positive economic data and a weaker US dollar. As the world's most traded currency pair, EUR/USD is a key indicator of global market sentiment and is closely watched by traders and investors alike.

Market Overview

The euro has been gaining strength against the US dollar in recent weeks, as economic data from the Eurozone continues to show signs of recovery. In particular, the recent release of Eurozone GDP figures showed a growth of 2.2% in the second quarter of 2021, surpassing expectations and indicating a strong rebound from the pandemic-induced recession. This has helped to boost confidence in the euro and push the EUR/USD pair higher.

Technical Analysis

From a technical perspective, the EUR/USD pair is currently trading above its 5-day high of $1.19 and is well supported at the $1.18 level. The 5-day low of $1.18 also serves as a key support level, indicating a strong level of buying interest at this price. On the upside, the $1.19 resistance level remains a key barrier for the pair, with a break above this level likely to lead to further bullish momentum.

Trading Outlook

With the bullish trend in place and strong support at $1.18, the outlook for the EUR/USD pair remains positive. However, traders should be aware of potential resistance at $1.19 and should monitor economic data releases from both the Eurozone and the US for any potential shifts in market sentiment. In the short-term, a trading strategy focused on buying on dips towards the $1.18 support level may be appropriate, with a target of $1.19 on the upside.
