Market Overview

The USD/JPY is one of the most actively traded currency pairs in the Forex market. The value of the Japanese Yen is heavily influenced by the policies of the Bank of Japan, while the US Dollar is affected by the decisions of the Federal Reserve. The current bullish trend of the USD/JPY can be attributed to the recent strengthening of the US economy and the potential for a future interest rate hike by the Federal Reserve.
