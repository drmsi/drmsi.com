GBP/USD Trading Analysis: Bullish Trend Continues

The GBP/USD pair, also known as Cable, has been on a steady uptrend in recent weeks, reaching a current price of $1.35. This marks a 0.33% increase in the past 24 hours and a 5-day high of $1.35. With a bullish trend in place, traders are wondering what the future holds for this popular Forex pair.

Market Overview

The GBP/USD pair has been on an upward trajectory since the beginning of October, with a strong support level at $1.34. The recent surge in price can be attributed to a combination of factors, including positive Brexit developments and a weaker US dollar. With the UK and EU nearing a potential trade deal and the US facing political uncertainty, the outlook for the GBP/USD remains positive.

Technical Analysis

From a technical standpoint, the GBP/USD pair is currently trading above its 50-day and 200-day moving averages, indicating a strong bullish trend. The Relative Strength Index (RSI) is also in the overbought territory, further supporting the bullish sentiment. However, traders should keep an eye on potential resistance levels at $1.35 and $1.36, which could trigger a short-term correction.

Trading Outlook

Based on the current market conditions, the short-term outlook for the GBP/USD pair remains bullish. Traders should keep an eye on the support level at $1.34, as a break below this level could signal a potential trend reversal. However, if the pair continues to hold above $1.35, we could see a further uptrend towards the resistance level at $1.36.
