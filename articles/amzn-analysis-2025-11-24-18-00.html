Amazon (AMZN) Trading Analysis

Amazon (AMZN) has been a hot topic in the stock market as it continues to experience a bearish trend. As of the time of writing, the stock is currently trading at $225.02, with a 24-hour change of -3.37%. In this trading analysis, we will take a closer look at the current market overview, technical analysis, and trading outlook for AMZN.

Market Overview

Amazon has been a top-performing stock in the past few years, with a strong track record of growth and profitability. However, recent market conditions have caused a decline in its stock price, with a 5-day high of $230.20 and a low of $215.18. The stock has also been struggling to break through the resistance level of $230.20, while finding support at $215.18.

Technical Analysis

From a technical standpoint, AMZN is currently in a bearish trend. The stock has been trading below its 5-day moving average, indicating a potential downward momentum. Additionally, the Relative Strength Index (RSI) is currently at 44, suggesting that the stock is approaching oversold territory.

Looking at the support and resistance levels, it is important to note that the stock has been testing the $215.18 support level multiple times in the past few days. If the stock continues to struggle at this level, it may indicate a potential break below and a further decline in price. On the other hand, if the stock manages to break through the resistance level of $230.20, it could signal a shift in momentum and a potential uptrend.

Trading Outlook

Based on the current market conditions and technical analysis, the short-term trading outlook for AMZN is bearish. Traders should keep a close eye on the support and resistance levels, as well as any potential market news or events that could impact the stock's price.

For short-term traders, a potential strategy could be to wait for a break above the resistance level of $230.20 before considering a long position. On the other hand, a break below the support level of $215.18 could signal a short opportunity.
