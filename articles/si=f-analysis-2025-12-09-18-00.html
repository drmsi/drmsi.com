Silver (XAGUSD) Trading Analysis

Silver, also known as XAGUSD or SI=F, is a popular commodity in the trading market. As of March 2021, the current price of Silver is $60.73 with a 24-hour change of 6.84%. In this article, we will provide a comprehensive trading analysis for Silver, including its market overview, technical analysis, and trading outlook.

Market Overview

Silver has been on a bullish trend recently, with a 5-day high of $61.11 and a 5-day low of $56.30. It has shown strong resistance at $61.11 and support at $56.30. This indicates that there is a potential for a breakout towards the resistance level, but traders should also be cautious of a potential pullback towards the support level.

Technical Analysis

From a technical standpoint, Silver is currently trading above its 50-day and 200-day moving averages, which is a bullish signal. The Relative Strength Index (RSI) is also in the overbought territory, indicating a strong buying momentum. However, the Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD) indicator is showing a bearish crossover, suggesting a potential short-term correction.

Trading Outlook

Based on the technical analysis, we can expect Silver to continue its bullish trend in the short term, with a potential target price of $61.11. Traders should keep a close eye on the support and resistance levels, as a breakout or pullback could occur at any time. It is important to set stop-loss orders to manage risk and protect profits.
