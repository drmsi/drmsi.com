Market Overview

The gold market has been largely driven by economic uncertainty and volatility in other financial markets. As a safe-haven asset, gold tends to perform well during times of economic turmoil and serves as a hedge against inflation. With the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic and global economic recovery still uncertain, the demand for gold has remained strong, leading to its recent upward trend.
