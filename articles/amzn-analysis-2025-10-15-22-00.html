Amazon (AMZN) Stock Trading Analysis

Amazon (AMZN) is a leading e-commerce and cloud computing company that has been experiencing a bearish trend in the stock market. As of the current market close, AMZN is trading at $215.57, down 4.28% from the previous day. In this trading analysis, we will take a closer look at the technical outlook for AMZN, identify key support and resistance levels, and provide a short-term trading forecast.

Market Overview

Despite the recent dip in stock price, Amazon's financials remain strong. In the second quarter of 2020, the company reported a net income of $5.2 billion, up from $2.6 billion in the same quarter last year. Additionally, Amazon's revenue increased by 40% year-over-year, reaching $88.9 billion. However, the company's stock has been impacted by the overall bearish market sentiment and concerns over the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on consumer spending.

Technical Analysis

From a technical perspective, AMZN's stock is currently in a bearish trend. The stock has been trading below its 5-day moving average and is currently hovering around its 5-day low of $212.60. The 5-day high of $228.25 is now acting as a resistance level, while the $212.60 support level has been tested multiple times in the past week.

Trading Outlook

Based on the current technical analysis, we can expect AMZN's stock to continue trading within the range of $212.60 to $228.25 in the short term. If the stock breaks above the resistance level of $228.25, it could potentially reach the next resistance level of $235.00. On the other hand, if the stock breaks below the support level of $212.60, it could potentially reach the next support level of $205.00.
