AMZN: Bearish Trend Persists Despite Recent Bounce

Amazon (AMZN) has had a volatile week, with a 24-hour change of -2.59% and a current price of $222.01. Despite a brief bounce from the 5-day low of $221.50, the overall trend remains bearish for the stock.

Market Overview

Amazon has been facing increased competition in the e-commerce market, with companies like Walmart and Target ramping up their online presence. This has put pressure on Amazon's stock price, causing it to dip below the 5-day low of $221.50.

Technical Analysis

The technical outlook for Amazon is currently bearish, with the stock trading below its 5-day high of $232.42. The 5-day moving average is also trending downwards, indicating a potential continuation of the bearish trend.

The support level for Amazon is currently at $221.50, which has held strong in recent days. If this level is broken, the next support level to watch is at $215.00.

The resistance level for Amazon is at $232.42, which has been a difficult level for the stock to break through. If this level is surpassed, the next resistance level to watch is at $240.00.

Trading Outlook

Despite the recent bounce from the 5-day low, the overall trend for Amazon remains bearish. Traders should exercise caution when considering buying the stock, as it may continue to face pressure from increased competition in the e-commerce market.

Short-term traders may find opportunities to sell the stock near the resistance level of $232.42, while longer-term investors may want to wait for a more significant trend reversal before considering buying.
