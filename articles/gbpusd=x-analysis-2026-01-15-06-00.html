GBP/USD Trading Analysis: Bearish Trend Continues

The GBP/USD (GBPUSD=X) is a popular currency pair in the Forex market, representing the exchange rate between the British pound and the US dollar. As of August 2021, the current price of the GBP/USD is $1.34, with a 24-hour change of -0.08%. The trend for this pair has been bearish, with a 5-day high of $1.35 and a 5-day low of $1.34. In this trading analysis, we will take a closer look at the market overview, technical analysis, and trading outlook for the GBP/USD pair.

Market Overview

The British pound has been under pressure in recent weeks due to concerns over the UK's economic recovery from the COVID-19 pandemic and the potential impact of Brexit. On the other hand, the US dollar has been gaining strength as the Federal Reserve prepares to taper its asset purchase program and potentially raise interest rates in the near future. These factors have contributed to the bearish trend in the GBP/USD pair.

Technical Analysis

Looking at the technical indicators, the GBP/USD pair is currently trading below its 50-day and 200-day moving averages, indicating a bearish sentiment. The Relative Strength Index (RSI) is also in the oversold territory, further supporting the bearish outlook. In terms of support and resistance levels, $1.34 remains a key support level, while $1.35 is a strong resistance level for the pair.

Trading Outlook

Based on the market overview and technical analysis, the short-term trading forecast for the GBP/USD pair is bearish. Traders may consider short positions targeting the support level at $1.34, with a stop loss above the resistance level at $1.35. However, it is important to monitor any developments in the UK economy and the US Federal Reserve's policies, as these could impact the direction of the pair.
