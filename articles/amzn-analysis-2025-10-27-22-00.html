Trading Analysis for Amazon (AMZN)

Amazon (AMZN) is a highly popular and widely traded stock, known for its e-commerce dominance and innovative business strategies. As of the time of writing, the stock is trading at a current price of $226.97, with a 24-hour change of 4.85%. The trend for AMZN is currently bullish, with the stock hitting a 5-day high of $228.40 and a 5-day low of $216.52. In this trading analysis, we will take a closer look at the technical outlook for AMZN, as well as support and resistance levels, to provide a short-term trading forecast.

Market Overview

Amazon's stock has been on a steady uptrend for the past year, with a 52-week high of $2461.00 and a low of $1626.03. The company's strong financial performance, including a 20% increase in net sales in the first quarter of 2020, has been a key driver of its stock price. Additionally, Amazon's expansion into new markets, such as cloud computing and grocery delivery, has further boosted investor confidence in the company.

Technical Analysis

Looking at the technical indicators, AMZN's stock is currently trading above its 50-day and 200-day moving averages, indicating a bullish trend. The Relative Strength Index (RSI) is also in the overbought territory, at 72.17, suggesting that the stock may be overvalued at its current price. However, the Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD) is showing a bullish crossover, indicating potential further upside for the stock.

Trading Outlook

Based on the technical analysis, AMZN's stock is likely to continue its bullish trend in the short-term. The next key resistance level to watch is at $228.40, with potential for the stock to reach its 52-week high of $2461.00 in the longer term. On the downside, the support level at $216.52 should provide a strong base for the stock, with a potential for a bounce back towards the $228.40 resistance level.
