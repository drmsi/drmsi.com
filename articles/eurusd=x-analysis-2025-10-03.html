Market Overview

The EUR/USD is the most traded currency pair in the world, representing the value of the euro against the US dollar. As the world's two largest economies, any changes in economic data or monetary policies from the European Union or the United States can greatly impact the EUR/USD exchange rate.

Technical Analysis

Trading Outlook
