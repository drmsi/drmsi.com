GBP/USD Trading Analysis: Bullish Trend Continues as Price Reaches $1.36

The GBP/USD (GBPUSD=X) has been on a bullish trend, reaching a current price of $1.36 with a 24-hour change of 1.81%. This currency pair has been showing strong momentum, with a 5-day high of $1.36 and a low of $1.34. In this article, we will analyze the technical outlook, support/resistance levels, and provide a short-term trading forecast for GBP/USD.

Market Overview

The GBP/USD has been on an upward trend, supported by positive economic data from the UK and a weaker US dollar. The UK's economy has been showing signs of recovery, with the unemployment rate dropping to 4.7% and retail sales increasing by 2.1% in August. On the other hand, the US dollar has been under pressure due to concerns over the country's economic recovery and the ongoing trade tensions with China.

Technical Analysis

From a technical standpoint, the GBP/USD is currently trading above its 50-day and 200-day moving averages, indicating a bullish trend. The Relative Strength Index (RSI) is also in the overbought territory, suggesting that the pair may be overvalued and due for a correction. However, the bullish momentum is still strong, and the pair has not shown any signs of a significant pullback.

Looking at support and resistance levels, the GBP/USD has a strong support at $1.34, which has been tested multiple times in the past month. On the upside, the resistance at $1.36 has been broken, and the next major resistance level is at $1.38.

Trading Outlook

Based on the current technical and fundamental factors, the outlook for the GBP/USD remains bullish. However, traders should be cautious of a potential correction as the RSI is in the overbought territory. Short-term traders may look for buying opportunities on pullbacks towards the $1.34 support level, while long-term traders may hold their positions and look for a break above the $1.38 resistance level.
