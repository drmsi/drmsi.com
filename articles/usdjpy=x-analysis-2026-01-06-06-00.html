Market Overview

The USD/JPY has been on an upward trend for the past few weeks, with the US dollar gaining strength against the Japanese yen. This can be attributed to the positive economic data coming out of the US, including strong job growth and a rebound in consumer spending. On the other hand, the Japanese yen has been facing pressure due to the country's struggling economy and the Bank of Japan's dovish monetary policy.
