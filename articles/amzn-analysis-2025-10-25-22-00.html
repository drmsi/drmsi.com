AMZN (AMZN) Trading Analysis

Amazon (AMZN) has been a hot topic in the stock market lately, with its current price sitting at $224.21 and a 24-hour change of 5.24%. Market Overview

Amazon is a leading e-commerce and cloud computing company, with a market cap of over $1 trillion. The company's stock has been on a steady upward trend over the past few years, driven by strong revenue growth and dominance in the e-commerce market. In 2020, Amazon's stock price has continued to climb, reaching all-time highs despite the economic uncertainty caused by the COVID-19 pandemic.

Technical Analysis

Looking at the technical indicators, the Relative Strength Index (RSI) for AMZN is currently at 70, indicating an overbought condition. This could suggest that the stock is due for a correction in the short term. However, the Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD) is still showing a bullish trend, with the MACD line above the signal line. This could indicate that the uptrend for AMZN is still intact.

Support/Resistance Levels

The current support level for AMZN is at $213.59, which has held up during recent pullbacks. If the stock were to break below this level, the next support level to watch would be at $200. On the other hand, the resistance level for AMZN is at $225.40, which has acted as a strong barrier in the past. If the stock were to break above this level, the next resistance level to watch would be at $240.

Trading Outlook

Based on the technical analysis and support/resistance levels, the short-term trading outlook for AMZN is mixed. While the RSI suggests an overbought condition and a potential correction, the MACD and strong support level could keep the stock in an uptrend. Traders should closely monitor the stock's price action and consider setting stop-loss orders to manage risk.
