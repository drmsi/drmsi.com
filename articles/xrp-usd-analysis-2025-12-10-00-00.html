Market Overview

Ripple is a real-time gross settlement system, currency exchange, and remittance network created by Ripple Labs Inc. in 2012. It is built upon a distributed open source protocol, and supports tokens representing fiat currency, cryptocurrency, commodities, or other units of value such as frequent flier miles or mobile minutes. Ripple purports to enable "secure, instantly and nearly free global financial transactions of any size with no chargebacks."

Despite being one of the top cryptocurrencies in terms of market capitalization, Ripple has faced some challenges in terms of adoption and regulatory issues. However, with its partnerships with major financial institutions and growing interest from investors, Ripple's future looks promising.
